Management

Governance

A Policy Guideline has been prepared in consultation with Board Members, Consultants, Auditor and organization’s Lawyer - will provide working mechanism for NGO – Great Samaj Sevi Sanstha with its office at 18, Avas Vikas Colony, Shahjahanpur, U.P., India. This guideline will remain relevant for a larger period of time; however, flexibility has also been decided to modify and amend the guidelines if the need arises.

Board Functions

The NGO board will set policies and strategies in line with the agreed purposes, principles and scope of the organization. It will also set operational guidelines, work plans and budgets for the NGO and policy and programme support. It will assist the internal workings of the NGO by setting criteria for membership of, and appointing in collaboration with the donor partners, review panels and/or support groups. It may also establish a framework for monitoring and periodic independent evaluation of performance and financial accountability of activities supported by the NGO.
